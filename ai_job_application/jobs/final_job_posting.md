# Job Posting: Production Assistant  
**Location:** Los Angeles  
**Start Date:** June 2025  

## Introduction  
Join our dynamic team for an exciting TV production set in the heart of Los Angeles! We are seeking a motivated and enthusiastic Production Assistant to support all facets of our production process, ensuring smooth operations on set. If you have a passion for film and television and thrive in fast-paced environments, we want to hear from you!  

## Role Description  
As a Production Assistant, you will be a critical member of our team, assisting various departments to ensure the production runs seamlessly from start to finish. Your role is to provide organizational and logistical support, contributing to the overall success of our project.  

## Key Responsibilities  
- Assist with daily production activities and coordinate logistics on set.  
- Support directors, producers, and crew in fulfilling their needs during production.  
- Manage equipment and supplies, ensuring everything is in working order.  
- Facilitate communication between departments to enhance collaboration and efficiency.  
- Help with set preparation and breakdown, including props and wardrobe management.  
- Gather and maintain production paperwork, ensuring accurate records.  
- Perform other tasks as assigned by senior production staff.  

## Requirements  
- Prior experience in film or television production is preferred.  
- Internships or an educational background in media studies are a plus.  
- Familiarity with production equipment and basic technical proficiency is desired.  
- Ability to thrive in team settings and work with diverse individuals under pressure.  
- Strong organizational skills, attention to detail, and adaptability are essential.  

## Benefits  
- Competitive weekly pay.  
- Complimentary employee meals on set.  
- Healthcare coverage available.
